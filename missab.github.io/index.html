About Us

We've been making nails look their very best

Miss AB's TOUCH is a full service nail care located at Mankessim in the Central Region. We believe our customers deserve only the highest quality service in the most relaxing setting possible. Our team comprises professionals to help our clients look their very best at all times.You come to our salon to experience the knowledgeable staff services using only the best products available on the market. We strive to make every client feel unique & special. Not only do we provide the best beauty services, but we also create an environment where the stress in your life simply melts away. We offer a full range of services including manicures and pedicures, nail enhancements and more.Our prices are also very affordable for all clients.As a matter of fact,we plan to lower our pricing so that we can gain good grounds within our startup period.We’re known for our specialty nail care services.We shall also be involved in giving training to interested people who may want to venture into the nail industry. Even though starting a nail salon might look pretty easy to start,we intend to bring in strict professionalism that will make us stand out.We shall also be involved in the sponsoring of various programs that are hosted in the locality which we are located,shows like fashion shows, hair exhibitions, entertainment shows, amongst many others.
